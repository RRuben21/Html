Recipe For Sausage Potato Skillet

Ingredients


  • 2 sausage links
  • 1 table spoon of canola oil
  • 1 small onion,sliced
  • 1/2 of green pepper sliced
  • 1/2 of red pepper sliced
  • 3 potatoes sliced

Directions


  1. In large skillet cook sliced sausages with the canola oil until cooked
  2. Add the onions, the peppers, and then cook unitl they are tender
  3. Place potatoes and 2 cups of water in skillet, then wait until it boils
  4. Add desigered amount of salt and pepper, or any other type of seasoning you would like
  5. Once it begins to boil reduce heat and cover, let simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Drain water then serve
